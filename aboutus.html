The story of SRF Traders is inseparable from the journey of its founder, Mr. Sheik Mytheen With a career spanning over 33 years in the iron business, Mr. Sheik Mytheen is a veteran who understands the very "bones" of construction. His journey began three decades ago, mastering the complexities of metal and trade. Recognizing a critical need for high-quality support structures in the growing infrastructure sector, he expanded his expertise into the centering and scaffolding business 12 years ago. The Company Story:
Building the Foundation Since 2017

Founded officially under its current account in July 2017, SRF Traders was established to bridge the gap between heavy industrial iron supply and the specific needs of modern construction sites. Based in the heart of the industry, SRF Traders specializes in the buying and selling of scaffolding and centering items. While the company entity was formalized in 2017, it carries the weight of 45 years of combined industry experience (33 years in iron + 12 years in centering). Today, SRF Traders serves as a vital link in the construction supply chain, providing the essential frameworks that allow skyscrapers, residential hubs, and industrial plants to rise. We don't just sell equipment; we sell the stability that allows our clients to build with confidence.
